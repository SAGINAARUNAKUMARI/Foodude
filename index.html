How does it work?

Connect. Order. Deliver.

Connect with flatmates

Share your shopping list in one, two, three and your flatmate will get notified when in the supermarket.

Save time

No more need to go to the supermarket: your flatmate will bring the groceries right to your door. Time is money.

Earn credits

Your flatmate pick ups your groceries at the supermarket and earns 10% on your total bill in credits. Easy money, isn't it?
